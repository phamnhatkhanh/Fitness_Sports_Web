Consider This

To better your health, should you begin a workout routine or start a healthy diet? The answer is both! Studies by the International Journal of Obesity show that a strategic combination of a healthy diet and exercise will provide more benefits over doing just one of the two. The combination will yield greater weight loss, fat loss, and a decrease in BMI, as well as help blood pressure related issues and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease!
